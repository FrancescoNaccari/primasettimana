Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topprd with sweet Caramelized onions, tart craberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oli, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oli, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough Ingredients until the floor is fully absordeb. you don't need to actuakly knead the dough,though I sometimes Knead it a few times in the bowl wehn mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
